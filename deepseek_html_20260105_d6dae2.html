About Grand Luxury Hotel

Discover our story, values, and commitment to providing exceptional hospitality experiences.

Our Story

Established in 1995, Grand Luxury Hotel has been at the forefront of hospitality excellence for over 25 years. What began as a single property has grown into an internationally recognized brand, known for our unwavering commitment to luxury, service, and guest satisfaction.

Our founder, Alexander Grand, envisioned a hotel that would redefine luxury hospitality. Today, we continue his legacy by combining timeless elegance with modern comforts, creating unforgettable experiences for our guests.
